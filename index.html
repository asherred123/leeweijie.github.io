About me

Hello! My name is Wei Jie, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Design at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). Expected to graduate in May 2026, I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge in real-world projects.

I am deeply passionate about cybersecurity and enjoy developing web pages for fun. In my free time, I enjoy participating in Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, which help me to continuously improve my skills and stay updated with the latest in cybersecurity. My experience in designing, developing, and managing projects has equipped me with a strong foundation in both technical and soft skills. I am always looking to learn new technologies and improve my skills, driven by a commitment to excellence and a desire to make a positive impact through my work.

As you can tell from this website, I am also a fan of donuts and enjoy making stuff. I am always looking for new opportunities to collaborate and create, so feel free to reach out if you have any exciting projects or opportunities to share!
